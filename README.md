# Welcome

This is a central place for my personal notes I have been taking while studying Japanese. I have chosen to make these notes public and share them as I am a strong believer that learning and the resources required for learning should be as accessible as possible to as many people as possible. In the event that my notes may be helpful to anyone else learning Japanese, then sharing them has been worthwhile.

> [!CAUTION]
> Everything here is subject to my poor understanding of Japanese and could very well be wrong. Take this all with a grain of salt.
> This is very much a work in progress and I am very much a beginner to Japanese.
