# いきます　と　きます

* `いきます` -（行きます）To suggest going somewhere the speaker is not currently.
* `きます` -（来ます）To say to come where the speaker in currently.
~~* Case 3 - `きます` -（来ます）To talk about where you went / general habits.~~

In the first 2 cases the pair of term are used from the perspective of the speaker, therefore いきます is used when the speaker is talking about going to somewhere they are not currently. きます is used when the speaker is talking about coming to the location they are currently at.
