# て Form 皆の日本語

> [!WARNING]
> `皆の日本語` teaches vocabuly based on the `ます Form` rather than the dictionary form. IMHO this is a bad way to learn vocabulary.
>
> This is relevant here because `皆の日本語` teaches conjugation to `て Form` from the `ます Form` rather than the dictionary form. This introduces unessasary steps to conjugation and created some ambiguity.
>
> This page only exists because the course I am doing uses `皆の日本語` and thus I have to learn this bad way of conjugation. I strongly reccomend lerning `て Form` conjugation from the dictionary form. see [て-form-conjugations.md](て-form-conjugation.md).

The following all have conjugations into the `て Form`:

* Verbs
* い-Adjectives
* な-Adjectives
* Nouns

## Conjugating Verbs

~~The way `verbs` conjugate depends on the type of verb. For how to identify Verb type please see [verb-types](verb-types.md).~~

`皆の日本語` does away with the idea of `ichidan` and `godan` verbs (why??). And just goes for `group 1`, `group 2`, `group 3`. `皆の日本語` assumes you are working with `Verbs` in the `ます Form` so this page will assume the same.

### Group 1

Group 1 seems to bascially just be `Godan Verbs` but with a set of modified rules for conjugations to make it work from the assumed `ます Form`.

#### Group 1 Verbs Ending in います　ちます　ります

 ending `います`, `ちます` or `ります` is replaced with `って`:

* `買います` --> `買って`
* `もらいます` --> `もらって`

#### Group 1 Verbs Ending in　みます　びます　にます

The ending `みます`, `びます` or `にます` is replaced with `んで`:

* `飲みます` --> `飲んで`
* `よみます` --> `よんで`

#### Group 1 Verbs Ending in します

The ending `します` is replaced with `して`:

* `話す` --> `話して`
* `殺す` --> `殺して`

#### Group 1 Verbs Ending in きます

The ending `きます` is replaced with `いて`:

* `書く` --> `書いて`
* `追いつく` --> `追いついて`

#### Group 1 Verbs Ending in ぎます

The ending `ぎます` is replaced with `いで`:

* `急ぎます` --> `急いで`
* `泳ぎます` --> `およいで`

### Group 2

Group 2 seems to be basically for `Ichidan Verbs`.

Group 2 verbs are easy to conjugate into thier `て Form`. The ending `ます` is simply replaced with `て`.

So `寝ます` becomes `寝て`.

### Group 3

Group 3 seems to basically be for `Irregular Verbs`

As there are only 2 Irregular verbs, its best to just remember thier conjugation:

* `します` --> `して`
* `きます` --> `きて`
