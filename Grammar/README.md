### Particles

| Gramma Point         | Example                                                     | Translation                          | Short Explanation                                                         |
| -------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| は (Subject)          | わたし**は** Aです                                           | I am A                                | Marks the topic/main subject of the sentence.                            |
| が (Subject)          | わたしは うま**が** すきです                                  | I like Horses                         | Marks a secondary subject or emphasizes the subject.                      |
| を (direct Object)    | おさけ**を** のみますか                                       | Do you drink Alcahol?                 | Marks the direct object of a verb.                                       |
| も (Also)             | Aさんは オーストラリアじんです。Bさん**も** オーストラリアじんです  | A is Australian. B is also Australian | Means "also" or "too". Can replace が and を directly.                    |
| の (Ownership)        | それは Aさん**の**です                                        | That belongs to A                     | Shows possession or ownership.                                           |
| の (Noun Substitute)  | たべものは からい**の**は にがてです                            | I dislike spicy food                  | Replaces a noun already understood from context. In this case **たべもの** |
| の (Place Relativity) | いす**の** うえに ねこがいます                                 | There is a cat on the chair           | Indicates location relative to another object.                            |
| に (Time)             | １２じ**に** おきました                                       | I woke up at 12                       | Marks a specific point in time.                                           |
| に (Frequency)        | 1年**に** 5回ぐらい 映画をみます                               | I watch about 5 movies a year         | Indicates frequency within a time period.                                 |
| に (Purpose)          | 渋谷へ お酒を のみ**に** いきました                             | I went to Shibuuya to drink          | Indicates purpose of movement.                                            |
| と (And)              | ワイン**と** ビールを のみます                                 | I drink wine and beer                 | Connects nouns ("and").                                                   |
| と (With)             | だれ**と** 東京へ いきましたか                                 | Who did you go to Tokyo with?         | Indicates doing something with someone.                                   |
| へ (Direction)        | きょうと**へ** 行きます                                       | I am going to Kyoto                   | Indicates direction or destination.                                       |
| で (Method)           | しんかんせん**で** いきます                                    | I will go via Bullet Train            | Indicates means or method.                                                |
| で (Location)         | えき**で** しんぶんを かいます                                 | I buy newspaper at the station        | Marks location where an action occurs.                                    |
| で (Tool)             | はし**で** たべました                                         | I ate using chopsticks                | Marks the tool used for an action.                                        |
| で (Group Selection)  | 日本の料理**で** 一番すきですか                                 | What is your favourite Japanese dish? |  Indicates selection from a group.                                        |
| で (Duration)         | にじかん**で** ほんを よみました                                | It took 2 hours to read a book        | Indicates how long an action took to complete.                            |
