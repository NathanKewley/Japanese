# て Form

The following all have conjugations into the `て Form`:

* Verbs
* い-Adjectives
* な-Adjectives
* Nouns

`Verbs` and `い-Adjectives` are modified when being conjugated into thier `て Form`.

For `な-Adjectives` and `Nouns` `で` is simply appended.

> [!NOTE]
> There is a table at the bottom of this page with examples of each and a summary of the rules to conjugate.

## Conjugating Verbs

The way `verbs` conjugate depends on the type of verb. For how to identify Verb type please see [verb-types](verb-types.md).

### Ichidan Verb Conjugation

Ichidan verbs are easy to conjugate into thier `て Form`. The ending `る` is simply replaced with `て`.

So `見る` becomes `見て`.

### Godan Verb Conjugation

This is the part that has no logic to it. The conjugation pattern depends on the ending of the `Godan Verb`. You kinda just need to memorise these rules... Yet another thing you just have to know.

#### Godan Verbs Ending in う　つ　る

The ending `う`, `つ` or `る` is replaced with `って`:

* `会う` --> `会って`
* `切る` --> `切って`

#### Godan Verbs Ending in　ぬ　ぶ　む

The ending `ぬ`, `ぶ` or `む` is replaced with `んで`:

* `死ぬ` --> `死んで`
* `読む` --> `読んで`

#### Godan Verbs Ending in す

The ending `す` is replaced with `して`:

* `話す` --> `話して`
* `殺す` --> `殺して`

#### Godan Verbs Ending in く

The ending `く` is replaced with `いて`:

* `書く` --> `書いて`
* `追いつく` --> `追いついて`

#### Godan verbs Ending in ぐ

The ending `ぐ` is replaced with `いで`:

* `泳ぐ` --> `泳いで`
* `注ぐ` --> `注いで`

### Irregular Verb Conjugation

As there are only 2 Irregular verbs, its best to just remember thier conjugation:

* `する` --> `して`
* `くる` --> `きて`

## Conjugating い-Adjectives

There are 2 steps required to conjugate an `い-Adjective` into its `て Form`

1. Conjugate to the `く` form by replacing the final `い` with `く`. (Read more about `く` form [here](nolinkyet.md))
1. Stick `て` straight onto the `く` form of the word.

* `難しい` --> `難しく` --> `難しくて`
* `美味しい` --> `美味しく` --> `美味しくて`

## Conjugating な-Adjectives

Easy, just append `で`!

* `有名` --> `有名で`
* `親切` --> `親切で`

## Conjugating Nouns

Also easy, just append `で`!

* `冷蔵庫` --> `冷蔵庫で`
* `駅` --> `駅で`
