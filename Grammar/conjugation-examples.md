## ます Form (Verbs)

| Term        | Type         | Verb Stem | Conjugation Type | ます Form       | Rule Summary                                              |
| ----------- | ------------ | --------- | ---------------- | -------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| たべる       | Ichidan Verb | たべ      | Present          | たべます        | `ます` is appended to the `Verb Stem`                      |
| たべる       | Ichidan Verb | たべ      | Negative         | たべません      | `ません` is appended to the `Verb Stem`                    |
| たべる       | Ichidan Verb | たべ      | Past             | たべました      | `ました` is appended to the `Verb Stem`                    |
| たべる       | Ichidan Verb | たべ      | Past Negative    | たべませんでした | `ませんでし` is appended to the `Verb Stem`                |

> [!NOTE]
> Only Ichidan Verbs are shown in the examples, however once you have the `Verb Stem` the rules are the same regardless or Ichidan, Godan or Irregular Verbs.

## Negative Form Adjectives

| Term        | Term (Kanji) | Type         | Negative Form    | Rule Summary                                               |
| ----------- | ------------ | ------------ | ---------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| さむいです   | 寒いです      | い-Adjective | 寒くないです      | The last `い` is replaced wtih `くない`                     |
| いいです     | いいです      | Exception    | よくないです      | Exception that you just have to know                       |
| きれいです   | 綺麗です      | な-Adjective | 綺麗じゃありません | `じゃありません` is appended to the end (in place of `です`) |
| ゆうめいです | 有名です      | な-Adjective | 有名じゃありません | `じゃありません` is appended to the end (in place of `です`) |

For detailed explanation on Adjective conjugations please see the [Main Page for い and な Conjugations](Adjectives-い-And-な-conjugations.md)

## て Form

| Term     | Type         | て Form    | Rule Summary                                                 |
| -------- | ------------ | --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 見る     | Verb/Ichidan | 見て       | The final `る` is replaced with `て`                          |
| する     | Exception    | して       | Exception you just have to remember                           |
| くる     | Exception    | きて       | Exception you just have to remember                           |
| 会う     | Verb/Goadn   | 会って     | Godan Verbs Endings `う`, `つ` or `る` is replaced with `って` |
| 死ぬ     | Verb/Goadn   | 死んで     | Godan Verbs Endings `ぬ`, `ぶ` or `む` is replaced with `んで` |
| 殺す     | Verb/Godan   | 殺して     | Godan Verbs Ending `す` is replaced with `して`                |
| 追いつく | Verb/Godan   | 追いついて  | Godan Verbs Ending `く` is replaced with `いて`                |
| 泳ぐ     | Verb/Godan   | 泳いで     | Godan Verbs Ending `ぐ` is replaced with `いで`                |
| 美味しい | い-Adjective | 美味しくて  | The final `い` is replaced with `くて`                         |
| 暑い     | い-Adjective | 暑くて     | The final `い` is replaced with `くて`                         |
| 有名     | な-Adjective | 有名で     | `で` is appended to the end of the `な-Adjective`              |
| 冷蔵庫   | Noun         | 冷蔵庫で　 | `で` is appended to the end of the `Noun`                      |

For detailed explanation of `て` form conjugations please see the [Main Page for て Form Conjugations](て-form-conjugation.md)

## て Form (皆の日本語)

> [!WARNING]
> This is not the advised way to conjugate into `て Form`.

| Term     | Type                   | て Form    | Rule Summary                                                   |
| -------- | ---------------------- | --------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 寝ます   | (Group 2) Verb/Ichidan | 寝て       | The ending `ます` is replaced with `て`                         |
| します   | (Group 3) Exception    | して       | Exception you just have to remember                             |
| きます   | (Group 3) Exception    | きて       | Exception you just have to remember                             |
| 買います | (Group 1) Verb/Goadn   | 買って     | The Ending `います`, `ちます` or `ります` is replaced with `って` |
| 飲みます | (Group 1) Verb/Goadn   | 飲んで     | The ending `みます`, `びます` or `にます` is replaced with `んで` |
| 殺します | (Group 1) Verb/Godan   | 殺して     | The ending `します` is replaced with `して`                      |
| 書きます | (Group 1) Verb/Godan   | 書いて     | The ending `きます` is replaced with `いて`                      |
| 急ぎます | (Group 1) Verb/Godan   | 急いで     | The ending `ぎます` is replaced with `いで`                      |
| tbd     | い-Adjective           | tbd        | tbd                         |
| tbd     | い-Adjective           | tbd        | tbd                         |
| tbd     | な-Adjective           | tbd        | tbd              |
| tbd     | Noun                   | tbd　      | tbd                      |

For detailed explanation of `て` form conjugations for 皆の日本語 see [Main Page for 皆の日本語 て Form Conjugations](て-form-conjugation-皆の日本語.md)
