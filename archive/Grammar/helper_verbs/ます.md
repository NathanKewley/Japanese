# Conjugations ます Verb Form

`ます Form` can be thought of as the polite or formal form of Japanese. `ます` itself is a `Verb` that is appended as a `Helper Verb`.

`ます Form` itself has variations for present, past, negative and negative past. All of which are covered here.

> [!WARNING]
> The `ます Form` is often the first thing someone will learn when learning Japanese like in the case with the `みんなにほんご` textbook. However I stongly believe its better to learn the `Dictionary form` of words first and that is what is assumed here.

## Conjugating into ます Form

Please see [conjugations v2](../conjugating/conjugations2.md).

## Negative Tense

Negative form takes present `ます Form` and swaps `ます` for `ません`

| Dictionary Form | ます Negative |
| --------------- | ------------ |
| たべる          | たべません    |
| たべす          | はなしません  |

## Past Tense

Past form takes present `ます Form` and swaps `ます` for `ました`

| Dictionary Form | ます Past    |
| --------------- | ------------ |
| たべる          | たべました    |
| たべす          | はなしました  |

## Past Negative Tense

Past negative form takes the `Negative ます` form and appends `でした` to the ending `ません`

| Dictionary Form | ます Past Negative |
| --------------- | ------------------ |
| たべる          | たべませんでした    |
| たべす          | はなしませんでした  |
