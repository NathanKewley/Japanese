# せる - Causative Helper Verb

This is used to say someone caused a person or thing to perform an action. The causing of the action itself is an action.

* `猫を　のませた` - ((Subject) caused the cat to drink)

You can also use this in conjuction with the [に](../particles/に.md) `Target of an Action` function to indicate the actor performing/causing the change:

* `猫に　ビールを　のませた` - ((Subject) caused the cat to drink beer)
